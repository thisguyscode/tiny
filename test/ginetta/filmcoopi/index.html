Filmcoopi

Filmcoopi Zürich

A modern look for a classic brand

The Swiss film distributor Filmcoopi Zürich has been synonymous with top independent films since 1972: If you’re a film lover, you are likely familiar with their legendary trailer.

In 2017, Ginetta had the honor of creating Filmcoopi’s new online presence. The goal was to craft an attractive film platform with an elaborate catalog search function and a simple film booking process.

A complete film catalog

Filmcoopi Zürich’s reputation rests on the impressive selection of their film catalog. We wanted to make sure this important element was easy to navigate so we organized it into three main filter categories: Currently in Cinemas, Coming Soon, and All Films. If the visitor seeks a specific film, director, actor, or genre, the powerful search bar can be used at any time and yields results quickly.

Visual Elegance

Films are dynamic and visually exciting. It was important for our lead designer and developer that the new Filmcoopi website felt this way too. The finishing touches of the visual design, including animations, give the new website an artistic quality that fits Filmcoopi’s unique brand.

Animated hover effect

Cockpit CMS

The content management system of the new website was created using Cockpit, a headless CMS. The extensive film catalog in this project was a perfect use case for the api-driven concept of Cockpit. Not only does it provide an intuitive user interface for the content creators, it also afforded us full creative freedom in design and development.

Admin view of the CMS

Modern Tech Stack

To create such a state-of-the-art website, we made use of the newest technologies such as React, Babel, Webpack, styled-components , Storybook, and Next.js.

At Ginetta, we are already film lovers, so the topic of this project was undoubtedly exciting for us, but still the best part was our creative and close collaboration with Yves and his team at Filmcoopi Zürich.
